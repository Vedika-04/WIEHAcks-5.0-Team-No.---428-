Our Story: From Waste to Worth


"Innovation is innovation only when its sustainable"


How It All Started

  • Inspired by the idea of sustainability and innovation, we embarked on a journey to convert waste material into a valuable resource.

  • Recognizing the untapped potential within discarded items, we envisioned a platform where waste could be transformed into wealth.

  • Our mission expanded to not only selling art created from waste materials but also promoting the talented artists behind these creations.

  • Collaborating with government initiatives focused on environmental conservation, our efforts gained momentum and aligned with broader societal goals.

  • We aimed to provide artists with a platform to showcase their creativity while offering them opportunities to earn from their art.

  • Additionally, we sought to alleviate the struggle for raw materials by providing processed waste through our website, thus empowering artists and fostering a culture of sustainability.

How Our Website Works: Waste Recycling Process


1.Waste Pickup

We pick your waste at your doorstep, dedicated team will reach on time to your locality.

2.Waste Collection

We collect all kind of wastes like plastics, electronic Waste Recycling, papyer etc.


3.Processing

Once after the waste is been collected by the team immidiately the amount will transfered digitally or with cash to our customers.

4.Recycling

Transforming waste into valuable resources, conserving energy and raw materials, suppling it to artists and contributing to a sustainable, eco-friendly future.

5. Suppling

The processed & recycled waste is supplied to artists for creating their art.

6. Promoting

The art created by the artists is promoted through our website therefore resulting in source of income for artists.
